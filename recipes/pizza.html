Kabo's Pizza

Description

An easy to make homemade pizza by me

Ingredients

  • Half a cup of pizza sauce
  • 1 pizza dough
  • 230 grams of sliced mozzarella
  • 8 basil leaves
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup of cut cherry or italian tomatoes
  • A small pinch of red pepper flakes
  • Olive oil (drizzle to your hearts content)
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • Pepperoni or Ham or both (Put as much as you'd like)
  • 1 cup frozen peas (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 260Â°C
  2. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough.
  3. Top with the fresh mozzarella, Pepperoni or Ham, tomatoes and bake 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is browned.
  4. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and a pinch of red pepper flakes.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and serve.

Conclusion

A beautiful, easy and quick to cook pepporini or ham pizza or both pizza

Cooks Note: Put as much Olive oil as you would like to consume. You can add a couple more basil leaves if you want more of that flavour
